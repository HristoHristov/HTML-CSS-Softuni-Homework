Headings and Paragraphs

  • Heading Tags <h1> - <h6>
    • <h1>Heading 1</h1>
    <h2>Sub Heading 2</h2>
    <h3>Sub Heading 3</h3>
  • Paragraph Tags: <p>
    • <p>Tis is my first paragraph</p>
    <p>Tis is my second paragraph</p>
  • Sections: <div> and <span>
    •  	<div style="background: skyblue;">
 		Tis is a div
 	</div>
